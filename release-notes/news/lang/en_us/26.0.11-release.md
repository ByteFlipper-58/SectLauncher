## SectLauncher 26.0.11-release — UI polish and a clearer everyday experience

### 👤 Accounts and profile area
* Fixed an issue where the account block could sometimes show a broken gray cube instead of the player head.
* The account area now feels more stable on startup, with fewer odd empty states and less visual flicker.
* Adding, switching, and removing accounts now feels clearer thanks to better interface feedback.

### 🧩 Mods shop
* The shop now does a better job explaining why no results are being shown.
* Empty states are much clearer when the problem comes from active filters, incompatible game versions, or an unavailable provider.
* Overall, the catalog feels less silent and easier to understand when something does not match.

### 🆕 New bottom tasks bar
* A brand new bottom background tasks bar has been added for installs, imports, updates, and repairs.
* It is now much easier to see what the launcher is doing during longer operations instead of wondering whether it got stuck.
* Progress, errors, and completed actions are easier to read, making long-running flows feel clearer and more reliable.

### ✨ Overall interface feel
* The bottom tasks bar and its details now appear and disappear more smoothly.
* Task details expand upward over the content without making the current page feel jumpy.
* The launcher now feels softer, steadier, and more polished in everyday use.
